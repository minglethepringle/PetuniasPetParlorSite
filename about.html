Hi! I'm Emily.

Hi, my name is Emily Lupatkin and I am the proud owner of Petunia’s Pet Parlor. I have been an animal lover my entire life and have never gone a day without a furry friend in my home. After college, I worked as a Veterinary Technician at the prestigious Angell Memorial Medical Center in Boston for 10 years. Venturing on a new path, I decided to enroll in the Dapper Dawg School of Professional Dog Grooming where I discovered my love of grooming and embarked on a new adventure. This has allowed me to combine my artistic side with my love of all things furry.

Grooming is an important part of caring for your pet and keeping them healthy. At Petunia’s Pet Parlor, I have created a calming and relaxing cage-free atmosphere in my home salon to make sure your pet feels safe and loved during every step of the grooming process. With over 20 years in the pet industry, a goal of mine is to help educate my clients on the proper care of their cherished pets. This enables them to spot problems and potential health issues. I strive to get to know you and your pet so that I can accommodate any special needs and provide the level of unique attention that both you and your pet deserve. From the wiggly puppies to the more sensitive elderly, all dogs are treated with patience and love. Pups look forward to their next day of beauty and pampering at Petunia’s Pet Parlor!
